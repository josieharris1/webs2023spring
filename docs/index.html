  OUR STORY

Afraid of solo dining? Music for a Sushi Restaraunt only seats tables for one. Our restaraunt was built in 2022 to turn solo dining into a gratifying experience. Our weekly sushi menus feature a compiled set list of music that can be listened to with complimentary headphones while dining.

Music for a Sushi Restaraunt is a tribute to English singer and songwriter Harry Styles as an activist for inclusivity and equal human rights. Since his debut as a member of One Direction in 2010, Harry Styles has demonstrated continuous positivity, support for the LGBTQ+ community, and has built a fanbase of over 48 million people.
He has also broken gender stereotypes in the world of fashion, diversifying the fashion insustry and making clothing more androgynous, as shown by his 2019 feature on the cover of Vogue dressed in Gucci.

Located in the heart of the Upper East Side in Manhattan, Music for a Sushi Restaraunt is the perfect place to take youself out for a date. In order to maximize your experience at Music for a Sushi Restaraunt, we reccomend ordering a drink, plugging in your headphones, and letting the sushi speak for itself.

At the sushi menu below, click on our music-inspired food items for direct links to this weeks songs.
